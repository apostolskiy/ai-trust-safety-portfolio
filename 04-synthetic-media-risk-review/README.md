# Synthetic Media Risk Review

This project will analyze risks related to AI-generated and AI-manipulated media.

## Focus Areas

- AI-generated personas
- Fake endorsements
- Deepfake-style content
- Manipulated visuals
- Impersonation
- Deceptive advertising
- Romance scam patterns
- Political or reputational manipulation

## Perspective

This project combines visual communication experience with Trust & Safety risk analysis.
