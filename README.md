# AI Trust & Safety Portfolio

This portfolio documents my transition into AI Trust & Safety, Synthetic Media Risk, AI Evaluation, and Responsible AI.

My background is in creative strategy, branding, visual communication, copywriting, and content analysis. I am especially interested in how AI-generated content can become misleading, unsafe, biased, inappropriate, manipulative, or harmful in real-world contexts.

## Focus Areas

- AI Trust & Safety
- Synthetic media risk
- LLM evaluation
- Responsible AI
- AI red teaming
- Misinformation and manipulated media
- Platform integrity
- Content safety

## Portfolio Structure

### 01. AI Safety Vocabulary

A working glossary of key AI Safety, Trust & Safety, and Responsible AI terms.

### 02. Risk Taxonomy

A structured classification of AI-generated content risks, including harm categories, severity levels, and examples.

### 03. LLM Safety Evaluation

A practical project for testing AI model responses against safety, helpfulness, factuality, and appropriateness criteria.

### 04. Synthetic Media Risk Review

An analysis of risks related to AI-generated personas, fake endorsements, deceptive visuals, manipulated media, and impersonation.

### 05. AI Red Team Mini-Report

A structured red-team-style evaluation of AI behavior using adversarial prompts, edge cases, and misuse scenarios.
